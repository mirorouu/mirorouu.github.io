Chicken Pesto Pasta

Tasty chicken pesto pasta, perfect for a quick and easy meal at home with family. In this household, we hate creamy pesto sauce!

Ingrediants

  • 2 containers of 6½ oz tradional Pesto sauce
  • 1 box of rigatoni pasta noodles (16 oz)
  • 1 bag of shredded mozerella cheese (8 oz)
  • 1 container of pre-shredded rotisserie chicken, at least 5 oz OR shred 2 chicken breasts

Instructions

  1. boil 5 cups of water around slightly above medium heat. Add a dash of salt.
  2. Once water is boiling, add rigatoni pasta noodles. Cook for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. While pasta is cooking, shred rotisserie chicken if not using pre-shredded chicken.
  4. Once pasta is cooked, drain water and return pasta to pot on low heat.
  5. add shredded chicken onto pasta as if forming light layer ontop.
  6. once satisfied, add 1 container of pesto sauce, stir, then add another.
  7. once pesto sauce is fully mixed in, add shredded mozerella cheese. Add as much as you like. for me that is usually 2 light layers. Then stir until the cheese has melted.
  8. serve and enjoy! This serving should be enough for leftovers (if you don't eat it all!)
